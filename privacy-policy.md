# Privacy Policy for Galerext

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

### Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Galerext handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to the Galerext mobile application (the "App").

### No Data Collection

**Galerext does not collect, use, share, or store any personal data.**

- **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use this App.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase) that track your usage.
- **No Advertising:** The App contains no third-party advertisements. Therefore, no data is shared with ad networks.
- **No Internet Connection Required:** The App functions entirely offline. Because it does not connect to the internet, it is technically incapable of transmitting any data to us or any third party.

### App Permissions

To function as a photo gallery with text search, the App requires access to specific features on your device:

- **Device Photos**: Galerext requires access to your device's photos to display them in the gallery and to perform offline text searches. The app does not upload, modify, or share your photos. All processing is done locally on your device.

- **Notifications**: Local notifications are used solely to inform you about the status of text indexing. No personal data is collected or transmitted through these notifications.

### Children's Privacy

Galerext is safe for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13, we do not require parental consent.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
